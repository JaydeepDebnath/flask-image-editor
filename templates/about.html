About Edit Monkey

Your lightweight and modern image editing solution for everyday use.

What is Edit Monkey?

Edit Monkey is a simple yet powerful web-based image converter and editor built with Flask. Designed for speed, simplicity, and performance — it helps you quickly transform images without the need for heavy desktop tools.

Our Mission

We aim to make image conversion accessible to everyone. Whether you’re a developer, designer, or just need a quick conversion, Edit Monkey gives you a fast, secure, and intuitive experience.

Key Features

  • Convert images to PNG, JPG, or WEBP formats instantly
  • Apply grayscale effects effortlessly
  • 100% browser-based — no installations needed
  • Clean, responsive design for any device
  • Built with Flask & Bootstrap 5 for reliability and speed
